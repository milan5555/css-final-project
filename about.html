About Us

Amul Dairy is a leading dairy company in India, known for its high-quality and nutritious products. Founded in 1946, Amul has grown to become a household name, synonymous with trust and excellence in the dairy industry.

We source our milk directly from farmers, ensuring that our products are fresh and pure. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best dairy products, made with love and care.

At Amul, we believe in innovation and quality, constantly striving to improve our products and processes to meet the evolving needs of our customers. Join us in experiencing the goodness of Amul Dairy.
